## Analysis Plan

**After survey deadline:**

1. **Response Review** (2 hours)
   - Read all open-ended responses
   - Identify themes and patterns
   - Flag concerns for engineering team

2. **Metrics Calculation** (1 hour)
   - NPS score calculation
   - Average ratings by question
   - Satisfaction distribution
   - Platform-specific insights

3. **Report Generation** (1 hour)
   - Summary of findings
   - Top 5 strengths mentioned
   - Top 5 improvements needed
   - GA readiness recommendation
   - Platform-specific issues

4. **Decision Making** (2 hours)
   - Engineering team reviews report
   - P0 blockers identified and prioritized
   - Go/no-go decision for GA release
   - Post-GA feature prioritization

---

## GA Readiness Decision Tree

Based on survey responses:

**IF all of:**
- NPS > 30
- Average satisfaction > 4.0
- <5 unresolved P0/P1 bugs
- >80% say SkillMeat ready for GA

**THEN**: Go for GA release

**ELSE IF**:
- NPS > 0
- Average satisfaction > 3.5
- <10 unresolved P0/P1 bugs
- >60% say SkillMeat mostly ready

**THEN**: GA with known issues list

**ELSE**:
- Delay GA, address critical feedback first
